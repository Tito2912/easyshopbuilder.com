What you will find on the EasyShopBuilder blog

Our editorial team monitors Shopify releases, affiliate program changes and compliance updates to help merchants make confident decisions. Every feature guide is sourced from official release notes, Shopify documentation and feedback from stores we actively support.

  • Ecommerce strategy: POS tutorials, international expansion, omnichannel operations and financial steering.
  • SEO & performance: technical checklists, structured data playbooks and optimisation tips for Shop Pay checkout.
  • Practical case studies: platform comparisons, app audits and insights from Shopify Plus and B2B rollouts.

How to use these resources

Start by bookmarking the categories that matter most for your roadmap — compliance launches, point-of-sale rollouts, or automation projects. Each post includes an executive summary, a prioritised checklist and curated links to Shopify documentation so you can turn insights into action without second-guessing the source.

We also annotate every article with the impacted Shopify plans, estimated implementation times and the metrics you should track once changes go live. If you run into a scenario we have not covered yet, reach out through the contact details in the footer: we continuously add follow-up guides based on reader questions.
